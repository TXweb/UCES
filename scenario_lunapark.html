"Cleveland? Of all the places in the world, you want to go to Cleveland?" Cindy was incredulous.

"That's right!" said Billy. "1905 - the opening year of Luna Park, one of the great parks of that era."

"We were thinking," said Bobby, "that it would fun to help build it. As great as it was, there are some things that could be better."

"Right again!" repeated Billy. "After all, we've learned a lot about building parks. Besides," he added, "Cleveland was supposedly a great place back then. I would like to see it."

"Well, okay, Cleveland it is," the girls answered.

He pushed the lever on the Time Machine's panel to engage the force field, and it vanished into time. Our heroes had begun yet another adventure.
