Installation Instructions:
  1. Click the link or disk image above to begin the download or right-click on the link/image and select "Save Target As" from the menu. Save the installer file to the directory of your choice on your computer.
  2. Locate the installer file (wherever you saved it during the download) and close all other open programs and windows on your desktop.
  3. Launch the installer and follow the prompts:
    • Choose your installation language, then click "Next >"
    • Be sure you've closed any open applications or windows, then click "Next >"
    • Read the UCES License Agreement, click the checkbox to indicate your acceptance, then click "Next >"
    • Select the location where you have previously installed Rollercoaster Tycoon 2. UCES must be installed into the root RCT2 directory. Click the "Browse" button to change the default installation directory or click "Next >" to proceed.
    • You will be asked to verify the installation directory. Click "OK" to proceed.
    • Select the "Full" install and click "Next >"
    • Confirm that you want to begin the installation by clicking on "Next >"
    • When the installation has completed, click "Finish" to close the installer software.
  4. When the installation has finished, launch Rollercoaster Tycoon 2 (as you normally would open the game).
  5. UCES Scenarios will now be available in the game's scenario selection windows. In order to "activate" the custom UCES objects, you will need to open each scenario.
