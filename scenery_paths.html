Concrete Footpath		 Junya Boy


Concrete path notes from Junya Boy: When making the concrete path, there was one idea in mind: Decorative centers for Tarmac Paths and seemless connections between the queue, exit and connnecting pieces. I got the idea when i saw ja227's post saying he thought the tarmac path was too thin. I also got the idea for real concrete slabs and also realistic dueling queves (like American Eagle at Six Flags Great America where the two rides have one queue then split into two. It can be recreated with this. It is two seperate queues but appear as one.

When building this path make sure you know where you are building (mainly the queue) because when placed next to each other you will not be able to tell which direction it's going. The paths extend a full tile; even the stairs extend full tile and there are no fences on them. You can place [fences] yourself.
