Hey, where did everybody go?" asked Billy as he opened the Time Machine's cockpit.

The wind whistled over the desert, blowing sand around them. Off in the distance, Cindy spotted a huge tower. "Look!" she cried.

Bobby studied the map. "I think it's in that direction," he said, pointing to the left.

"No, silly," said Cindy pointing at the tower in front of them. Bobby checked the map again.

"Here, let me help," said Candy. She grabbed the map, turned it then handed it back. Bobby looked down at the map again. He studied it for a moment and said, "It must be straight in front of us."

Candy stifled a giggle. "Ten coasters! I bet a lot of peeps will show up if we build them ten coasters."

"Let's find out!" they all said together.
