Luna was painted in a rainbow of pinks, greens yellows, oranges, blues, and purples.  Luna Park was open until midnight, and every structure was outlined in more than sixty-thousand colored light bulbs.  A popular advertising slogan was "It’s Never Dark at Luna Park".
