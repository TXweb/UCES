October 15, 2003
Happy birthday, RCT2!

The game is one year old today. So what should we do to celebrate? How about a big party and lots of presents! If that's what you want, you've come to the right place - we've got a big, big party going on here! We've got enough goodies to give you that sugar rush you crave. The handymen are around here somewhere, so there's no need to hold back.

To answer the obvious question, we thought of a time travel storyline first - or at least, concurrently! Our storyline for "The Time Machine" was conceived in February, 2003. From the looks of things, our time machine is better than brand "A's." It goes to more exciting places, plus has a special button that ... wait! We can't spill all the beans just yet. Check out the world of the Time Machine and our ten Time Machine scenarios for the scoop.

But that's not all, tycooners! There's another whole storyline with seven additional scenarios included in this package. It's called "Katy's Dreamworld. It's the story of a young girl with a very special talent. Like many special things, this talent is a mixed blessing. Sometimes a gift is not free.

Now, it's time to get down to business! We know why you're here. There's a lot of great RCT2 content here - scenarios, shops, paths, scenery and rides! Go get 'em!

Oh, one more thing...from all of us in the UCES, Happy Tycooning!
